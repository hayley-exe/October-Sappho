Early Life

Sappho was born into an aristocratic family around 630 BCE on the Greek island of Lesbos. With her upbringing she was exposed to a wide range of intellectual and artistic influences. With her family's prominence in the political and cultural life of Lesbos facilitated her education in music, poetry, and dance.
